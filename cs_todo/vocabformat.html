VOCABULARY LIST FORMAT
======================

First line must contain "WaKan Word List 1"
Lines beginning with ; are comments
Blank lines are ignored

Each word is identified by four lines:
1st - reading of the word (Unicode in hex)
2nd - writing of the word (Unicode in hex)
3rd - english meaning
4th - category in the format ~
